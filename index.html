Pandora's Box!

What is it? Where did it originate? Symbolism?
It is a mythological story about Pandora who's opened a container (Pandora's Box) out of curiosity but the container instead released curses onto mankind. It is a greek mythological story originally from Heisod's 700 B.C poem Works and Days. It symbolizes the curiosity of mankind but as well the consequences of such curiosity.
